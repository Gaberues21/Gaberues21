# About me

I am a third-year PhD student in Applied Mathematics at the University of Manchester. My main research interests are acoustics, wave mechanics, and wave propagation in complex materials such as composites and metamaterials.

- I recieved an Undergraduate Degree in Engineering Sciences at the University Austral of Chile on 2021. Two years later I completed my MSc in Physics, especifically in Acoustics and Vibrations, at the same university. During the development of bachelor and master I started researching acoustic wave propagation in complex media, particularly porous composites and metamaterials with resonant units comprised of resonators and flexible membranes. This work entail deriving effective properties of such complex materials by means of mathematical methods such as the two-scale asymptotic method of homogenisation. Moreover, for my Master's thesis I built and measured the absorption properties of composites and metamaterials including heat sources.
